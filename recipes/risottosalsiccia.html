Risotto alla Salsiccia

Risotto with salsiccia is a tasty way to warm a cold winter night. With its creaminess and rich taste this risotto will tingle your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • Two sausages
  • Rice 150 gr
  • One onion
  • One liter of broth
  • Half a glass of white wine
  • Parimigano cheese to liking
  • One spoon of butter
  • Olive oil
  • Black pepper
  • Two small rosmary branches

Steps

  1. Chop and mince the onions, prepare the sausages by removing the skin.
  2. Warm the broth, add the onions, oil and sausages in a pan and cook them.
  3. After the ingredients in the pan are ready add the rice and broth.
  4. Keep stirring until the broth evaporates and the rice is creamy, add the butter.
  5. Let it cool down for a few minutes, decorate with rosmary branches and serve.
